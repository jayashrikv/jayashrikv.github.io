Sukku Vellam

Traditional Ginger-Jaggery Wellness Blend

Heritage & Wisdom

Sukku Vellam is a time-honored wellness preparation that brings together the warming properties of ginger with the natural sweetness of jaggery and the nourishing qualities of ghee. This traditional blend has been cherished for generations for its role in supporting digestive health and post-natal care.

Each batch is carefully crafted following traditional methods, ensuring the perfect balance of ingredients and their therapeutic properties.

Pure Ingredients

  • ✧ Premium dried ginger (Sukku), known for its digestive and warming properties
  • ✧ Traditional palm jaggery, rich in natural minerals
  • ✧ Pure cow's ghee, enhancing absorption and benefits

Traditional Benefits

  • Supports healthy digestion and metabolism
  • Particularly beneficial for post-natal care
  • Helps maintain body warmth and comfort
  • Provides natural energy and nourishment
  • Aids in maintaining digestive balance

Usage & Care

Take as recommended by your healthcare practitioner. For general digestive support, a small portion can be consumed after meals. During post-natal care, follow specific dosage guidance from your healthcare provider.

Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. The natural preservative properties of ghee and jaggery help maintain its quality.

Note: While this is a traditional supplement, please consult with your healthcare provider before use, especially during post-natal recovery.
