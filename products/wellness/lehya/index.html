Usage & Care

This precious formulation is primarily designed for post-natal care, though it can be used in limited quantities to support digestive health. Take as directed by your healthcare practitioner, typically a small portion twice daily.

Store in a clean, dry glass container away from direct sunlight. The natural preservative properties of honey and ghee help maintain its efficacy.

Note: This traditional supplement should be taken under proper guidance, especially during the post-natal period.
