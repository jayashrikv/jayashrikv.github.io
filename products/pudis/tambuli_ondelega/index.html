Ondelaga Tambuli Pudi

Brahmi / Water Hyssop Chutney Powder

Heritage & Craftsmanship

This exquisite chutney powder is a testament to ancient Ayurvedic wisdom, featuring the revered herb Brahmi (Water Hyssop). Known for its cognitive-enhancing properties and used in traditional medicine for centuries, Brahmi forms the heart of this carefully crafted blend.

Each batch is mindfully prepared using time-honored techniques, ensuring that the therapeutic properties of every ingredient are preserved. The result is a harmonious blend that not only delights the palate but also nurtures the body and mind.

Artisanal Ingredients

  • ✧ Sun-dried Ondelaga (Brahmi) leaves, hand-picked at optimal maturity
  • ✧ Premium coriander seeds, carefully roasted to release essential oils
  • ✧ Aromatic jeera (cumin), known for its digestive properties
  • ✧ Fresh black pepper, adding warmth and enhancing nutrient absorption
  • ✧ Select kobri (dried mature coconut), providing richness and body

Traditional Benefits

  • Supports cognitive function and memory enhancement
  • Helps reduce stress and anxiety naturally
  • Promotes mental clarity and focus
  • Aids in digestion and metabolism
  • Contains natural antioxidants for overall wellness

Usage & Storage

Mix a spoonful with fresh yogurt or buttermilk to create a traditional tambuli, or sprinkle directly over rice with a drizzle of ghee. This versatile powder can also be used as a seasoning for vegetables or incorporated into chutneys.

Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place to maintain its aromatic properties and therapeutic benefits.
