Pudina Pudi

(Mint Chutney Powder)

A refreshing and flavorful chutney powder that brings together the cooling properties of mint with traditional ingredients. This versatile powder adds a delightful twist to your daily meals.

Ingredients

  • Dried and slightly roasted mint leaves
  • Channa dal
  • Red chillies
  • Tamarind
  • Kobri (dried mature coconut)
