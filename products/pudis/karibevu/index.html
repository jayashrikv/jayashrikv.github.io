Karibevu Pudi

Curry Leaf Chutney Powder

Heritage & Craftsmanship

Discover the essence of traditional South Indian cuisine with our signature Karibevu Pudi. This aromatic blend celebrates the humble curry leaf, a cornerstone of Indian cooking known for its distinct flavor and remarkable health benefits.

Each batch is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that the delicate balance of ingredients creates a powder that's not just a condiment, but a celebration of our culinary heritage.

Artisanal Ingredients

  • ✧ Hand-picked curry leaves, dried naturally and lightly roasted to enhance flavor
  • ✧ Nutrient-rich urad dal, adding protein and creating a perfect texture
  • ✧ Premium black pepper, providing warmth and digestive benefits
  • ✧ Natural tamarind, offering a subtle tangy note
  • ✧ Fresh kobri (dried mature coconut), bringing richness and depth

Traditional Benefits

  • Supports healthy digestion and metabolism
  • Rich in antioxidants and essential nutrients
  • Known to promote hair health and vitality
  • Helps maintain healthy cholesterol levels
  • Natural source of iron and fiber

Usage & Storage

This versatile powder can be enjoyed in multiple ways - mix with hot rice and a drizzle of ghee, blend with yogurt for a nutritious raita, or use as a seasoning for vegetables and salads. Its balanced flavor profile makes it a perfect accompaniment for any meal.

Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place to maintain the aromatic properties of the curry leaves and the freshness of all ingredients.
