Ganike Tambuli Pudi

(Black Nightshade / Manathakkali Chutney Powder)

A unique and nutritious chutney powder featuring the traditional medicinal herb Ganike (Black Nightshade). This carefully crafted blend combines ayurvedic wisdom with delicious taste.

Ingredients

  • Dried Ganike leaves
  • Carefully roasted coriander seeds
  • Jeera (Cumin)
  • Pepper
  • Kobri (dried mature coconut)
