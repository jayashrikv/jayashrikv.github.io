Ganike Tambuli Pudi

Black Nightshade Chutney Powder

Heritage & Craftsmanship

Discover the profound healing wisdom of traditional Ayurveda with our Ganike Tambuli Pudi. This remarkable blend features the treasured Black Nightshade herb, known for its exceptional medicinal properties and used in traditional healing practices for generations.

Each batch is thoughtfully prepared using time-tested methods, ensuring that the therapeutic qualities of the ingredients are preserved in their purest form. The result is a harmonious powder that offers both nourishment and healing properties.

Artisanal Ingredients

  • ✧ Carefully dried Ganike (Black Nightshade) leaves, harvested at their peak potency
  • ✧ Aromatic coriander seeds, slow-roasted to enhance their natural oils
  • ✧ Premium jeera (cumin), selected for its digestive properties
  • ✧ Hand-picked black pepper, adding warmth and improving bioavailability
  • ✧ Fresh kobri (dried mature coconut), providing a rich, creamy texture

Traditional Benefits

  • Supports digestive health and alleviates stomach discomfort
  • Known for its natural anti-inflammatory properties
  • Helps boost immunity and overall vitality
  • Traditionally used for maintaining skin health
  • Provides relief from seasonal ailments

Usage & Storage

For a traditional tambuli experience, blend a spoonful with fresh yogurt or buttermilk. Alternatively, sprinkle over hot rice with a touch of ghee for a comforting meal. This versatile powder can also be used to enhance the nutritional value of various dishes.

Store in an airtight container away from direct sunlight to preserve its medicinal properties and aromatic essence.
