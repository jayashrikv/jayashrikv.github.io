Kottambri Chutney

(Fresh Coriander Chutney)

A fresh and aromatic chutney made from hand-picked coriander leaves. This traditional recipe uses cold-pressed groundnut oil and carefully selected ingredients to create a perfect accompaniment for your meals.

Ingredients

  • Freshly sourced coriander
  • Green chilli
  • Asafoetida
  • Cold-pressed groundnut oil
