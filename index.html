About the Chef

Hello! My name is Jayashri and I am a passionate advocate of traditional South Indian cuisine, deeply rooted in its rich heritage and nutritional wisdom. Having grown up in a traditional Iyengar household, I have spent decades mastering the art of cooking—not just learning recipes, but also understanding the medicinal benefits of every ingredient. With over 30 years of experience in running a home and raising three wonderful children, I have always believed that food is more than just sustenance—it is a source of nourishment, healing, and well-being inspired by generations of wisdom. Now, with some time to dedicate to my passion, I am on a mission to share the goodness of time-honored recipes with a wider audience. Every product I offer is freshly made upon order, ensuring authenticity, purity, and the highest nutritional value. Through this platform, I hope to bring you the essence of South Indian tradition—one that is rooted in purity, health, and the love of home-cooked food. Join me on this journey to rediscover the power of natural, nourishing ingredients, just the way our ancestors intended. Let’s celebrate the richness of traditional food, made with care and passed down through generations.
